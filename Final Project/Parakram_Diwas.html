Parakram Diwas

Parakram Diwas is an event that is to be celebrated in India in the honor of a great leader and freedom fighter Subhash Chandra Bose. It had been decided by the Government of India and announced by Union Culture Ministry on 19th January 2022 that the birthday of Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose will be observed as Parakram Diwas throughout the nation. The first celebration was held on 23rd January 2022 that was the 126th birthday of Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose.

The decision of celebrating Parakram Diwas has been taken by the government of India to remember and pay tribute to the contributions made by Netaji for the nation during the struggle for Independence. The celebration of Parakram Diwas will inspire the people of the nation and especially the younger
